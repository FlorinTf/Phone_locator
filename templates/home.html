Phone Tracker

{% include "banner.html" %}

This application generates information for any phone number, whether it is a landline or mobile. Even if separated by "." , " " or "/".
The number must be in international format with "+"-"Country code"+"number" Ex: +4073952...... .
For mobile phones, it generates the following information: country where the phone number is registered, continent,
country time, national currency, currency symbol, currency name, country code, latitude and longitude coordinates specific
to the country and display this location on the pop-up map .
And for landlines, it shows the city where the phone number is registered and the metropolitan area.
