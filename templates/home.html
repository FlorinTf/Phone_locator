Phone Tracker

{% include "banner.html" %}

This app generates information for any phone number whether it is a landline or a cell phone. Even if it is separated by "." or "-". The number must be in international format with "+" Ex: +40739......... For cell phones: country where the phone number is registered, continent, country time, national currency, currency symbol, currency name, country code, country-specific latitude and longitude coordinates and render this location on the popup map. For landlines, it shows the city where the phone number is registered and the metropolitan area.
